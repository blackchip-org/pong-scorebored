Rules

The Pong Scorebored implements the following rules:

  • Games can be either 11 or 21 points in length.
  • Matches can be a single game, best two out of three, best three out of five, or best four out of seven.
  • Rally scoring is in effect.
  • There is a volley for service in the first game of a match.
  • In subsequent match games, the loser of the previous game serves first.
  • In 21 point games, servers change after every 5 points.
  • In 11 point games, servers change after every 2 points.
  • A win must be by at least 2 points.
  • In overtime servers change after every point.
  • In 21 point games:
    • Winning all 5 points in a service is an Ofer
    • Two consecutive Ofers is a Kenfer
    • Three consecutive Ofers is a Turkey
    • Four consecutive Ofers is a Double Kenfer
  • In 12 point games an Ofer is awarded for winning both points in two consecutive services
  • In a 21 point game, Jacob is not impressed if you lose with 12 points or less.

Guide

When a match starts in the Scorebored screen, press the Left Arrow to award the service to the left team or press the Right Arrow to award the service to the right team.

During play, use the buttons at the bottom right hand of the screen or the following key shortcuts:

Key Action
Left Arrow Award a point to the left team
Right Arrow Award a point to the right team
Shift Left Arrow Subtract a point from the left team
Shift Right Arrow Subtract a point from the right team
Y When the ball dribbles over the net, press to say "Yeah Baby!"
O When the net rejects the ball, press to say "No Baby!"
H When the ball nicks the side of the table, press to say "Ssssssssssh!"
N On a serve that hits the net, press to say "Nothing but net!"
S Announces the score
V Announces who is serving
E Emit a random Jacob excuse
T Adjust the match settings
A Adjust the score or server
M Mute the announcer

At the end of the match, press either the left or right arrow key to return to the dashboard.
